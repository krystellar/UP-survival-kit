STUDENT TIPS

1. Manage your time effectively

Prioritize tasks and avoid procrastination. User a planner or digital calendar to track classes, assignments, and deadlines.

2. Get involved on campus

Join clubs, organizations, or sports team to meet new people and explore interests. Attend campus events to connect with your community.

3. Take care of your health

Maintain a balanced diet, exercise regularly, and get enough sleep. Utilize campus health services or counseling.

4. Develop good study habits

Find a study routine and quiet place to focus. Don't hesitate to seek help from professors and peers.

5. Budget and handle finances wisely

Keep track of your expenses and create a budget to avoid overspending. Take advantage of student discounts and free resources on campus.

6. Step out of your comfort zone

Be open to new experiences, friendships, and ideas. Don't be afraid to ask questions or seek advice - it's part of the learning process.
